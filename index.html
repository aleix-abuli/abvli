My name is Aleix Abulí and I’m an artist/web developer from Barcelona.

This site is a small recopilation of my art. As you will notice, I tend to gravitate towards fashion iconography, surrealism, body horror and science fiction, and my work is a little mix of everything that mainly takes shape in the form of illustrations, character designs and comic stripes.

My longterm goal would be to become a comic artist and get my work published, but for the moment I’m just focusing on having fun and expanding my artistic world and ideas.
